Lesson 7 — Careers in Computing

Computing careers are diverse: software engineer, data scientist, cybersecurity analyst, game developer, and more. This lesson introduces pathways into computing fields.

Activity 1: Career Match

Match interests to careers. For example: If you love storytelling and design, UI/UX design may be a great path.

If you enjoy solving mysteries and protecting systems, which career fits?


Activity 2: Guest Speakers or Videos

Introduce real-world computing professionals through guest talks or short videos to inspire students with real experiences.

Reflection Prompts

  • Which computing career excites you the most and why?
  • What skills do you already have that could help you in computing?

Wrap-Up & Transition

Computing offers a world of opportunities. Whether you build apps, secure systems, or analyze data, your journey can start today.

Next Steps: Keep exploring courses, internships, and projects that build your skills.
