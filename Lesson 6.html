Lesson 6 — Computing in the World

Computing is everywhere — from healthcare and education to entertainment and business. In this lesson, we explore how computing impacts industries and society.

Activity 1: Industry Spotlight

Pick an industry: healthcare, sports, or music. Discuss how computing is used there. For example, in healthcare, AI helps doctors read X-rays.
